About CivicTech
Waterloo Region

CivicTechWR focuses on using technology to improve how we connect with our local communities and governments. It's about making civic interactions and services more efficient and effective. CivicTech Waterloo Region was founded in July 2017. We're a community group that brings together people from diverse sectors and industries to collaboratively solve challenges facing our local community using design and technology. We were inspired by similar organizations like CivicTechTO, Beta City YEG, and Code for America Brigades.
